Can you download ArdaCraft's map?

ArdaCraft is a pretty cool world, we like to think, and seeing as you’ve clicked on this article the chances are that you think so too. Do you have a dodgy internet connection and want to be able to explore Middle-Earth offline? Do you want to show it to your friends and pretend you built it yourself? Or maybe there’s a whole other reason you want to access our creation without joining the server. So can you download ArdaCraft’s map?

Short answer

No (sorry)

Long answer

Our downsized recreation of Tolkien’s fictional continent is still pretty big; coming in at roughly the size of Luxembourg (Rhode Island for the Americans), which makes for a massive world size that would be incredibly impractical to download for most people. Additionally, our world is not static: everyday our members are building, destroying and otherwise improving the project, meaning that your download would be instantly outdated and of an unfinished project.

Fear not though; our server is free to join with fast load times and an active and welcoming community of builders. So what are you waiting for? Hop online and join us.
